TimeWeave+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Data Collection

TimeWeave+ collects the following types of data to provide app functionality:

  • Location Information: Used for location-based reminder triggers. Location data is only used when you set up location reminders and is not continuously tracked.
  • Contact Information: Used for contact-based reminder triggers. We only access contacts you specify and do not upload or share your contact list.
  • User-Created Content: Including tasks, reminders, notes, etc. This content is stored on your device.
  • Device Identifiers: Used for app functionality such as notification management and data synchronization.
  • Usage Data: In-app activity data used to improve user experience.

2. Data Usage

We use collected data only for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality (reminders, task management, etc.)
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Providing customer support

We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored in the following locations:

  • Local Storage: All task and reminder data is stored on your device.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted and stored in your iCloud account. Only you can access this data.

We do not store your personal data on our servers.

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Using encryption and security mechanisms provided by iOS
  • iCloud data uses end-to-end encryption
  • We do not collect sensitive information (such as passwords, payment information, etc.)

5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Right: You can view all your data in the app at any time
  • Deletion Right: You can delete data in the app at any time
  • Export Right: You can export your data through iCloud or other methods
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke app permissions (location, contacts, etc.) in iOS Settings

6. Third-Party Services

TimeWeave+ may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud: For data synchronization (optional feature)
  • Apple Maps: For location search and map display

Use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

TimeWeave+ is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you in the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
