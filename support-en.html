Q: How do I create a thread (reminder)?
A: Tap the button in the top right corner of the main screen, fill in the thread title and select a time, then tap "Save". You can also set repeat rules, priorities, location triggers, and other advanced options.
Q: How do I set up recurring reminders?
A: When creating a reminder, select your desired repeat type (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.) in the "Repeat" option. You can also choose "Custom" to set more complex repeat rules, including the Ebbinghaus forgetting curve mode.
Q: How do location-based triggers work?
A: When creating a reminder, find "Location Trigger" in "Advanced Options", tap it and select a location (you can search or select on the map), then choose the trigger type (arrival or departure). The app will automatically remind you when you arrive at or leave that location.
Q: What is the Time Letter feature?
A: Time Letters allow you to write to your future self. Create a letter, set a delivery time, and when the time arrives, you'll receive the letter. This is a unique feature for recording important moments and reflecting on your growth journey.
Q: How do I complete a task?
A: Swipe right on the task card to complete it. You can also tap the task card and then tap the "Complete" button. If the task is set to Ebbinghaus mode, it will automatically reschedule based on the forgetting curve after completion.
Q: How do I postpone a task?
A: Swipe left on the task card to postpone it by 10 minutes. You can also tap the task card and then tap the "Postpone" button.
Q: How do I use Widget?
A: Long press on the home screen, tap the "+" button in the top left corner, search for "TimeWeave+", select your desired widget size (small, medium, or large), and add it to your home screen. The widget will automatically display today's tasks.
Q: How do I change the language?
A: Open the app, go to the "Settings" page, find the "Language" option, and select your preferred language (Simplified Chinese or English). The app will automatically refresh the interface language.
Q: Will my data sync to the cloud?
A: Current version stores data locally on your device. If your device has iCloud backup enabled, data will be included in the backup. We are developing iCloud sync functionality, which will be released in a future version.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Create Your First Thread: Tap the button in the top right corner, fill in the title and time, then tap "Save"
  2. Set Repeat Rules: If you need recurring reminders, select a repeat type when creating
  3. Complete Tasks: Swipe right on the task card to complete
  4. Postpone Tasks: Swipe left on the task card to postpone by 10 minutes
  5. Edit Tasks: Tap the task card to edit or delete tasks

Feature Details

  • Smart Reminders: Support for one-time and recurring reminders, including custom repeat rules
  • Location Triggers: Automatically remind when arriving at or leaving specific locations
  • Time Letters: Write to your future self, receive at specific times
  • Task Management: Support for subtasks, attachments, priorities, and categories
  • Repeat Rules: Support for daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, weekdays, custom repeats, and Ebbinghaus forgetting curve mode
  • Widget Support: Quickly view today's tasks on the home screen

Usage Tips

  • Set different priority colors for different types of tasks for quick identification
  • Use location triggers for shopping lists, automatically remind when arriving at the store
  • Use Ebbinghaus mode for review and study, scientifically schedule repeat times
  • Use Time Letters to record important moments, write letters to your future self
  • Use subtasks to break down complex tasks into multiple steps
  • Add attachments (photos, videos, audio) to make tasks more rich
